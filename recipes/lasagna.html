Lasagna Recipe


Description

  This lasagna recipe, titled World's Best Lasagna, is a popular and long-standing recipe, with over 7 million views annually. It combines a rich blend of sweet Italian sausage, lean ground beef, and a flavorful tomato-based sauce. The recipe is cherished for its meaty, cheesy layers, enhanced by a mix of Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheeses.

Ingredients

  • Meat:  Sweet Italian sausage, lean ground beef
  • Vegetables & Herbs:  Onion, garlic, fresh parsley, dried basil, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds
  • Tomato Products:  Crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste
  • Cheese:  Ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan
  • Other:  Egg, sugar, lasagna noodles
