Biryani Recipe


Description

  This Biryani recipe, titled World's Best Biryani, is a popular and long-standing recipe, with over 7 million views annually. It combines a rich blend of sweet Italian sausage, lean ground beef, and a flavorful tomato-based sauce. The recipe is cherished for its meaty, cheesy layers, enhanced by a mix of Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheeses.

Ingredients

  • Meat:  Sweet Italian sausage, lean ground beef
  • Vegetables & Herbs:  Onion, garlic, fresh parsley, dried basil, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds
  • Tomato Products:  Crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste
  • Cheese:  Ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan
  • Other:  Egg, sugar, Biryani noodles

Steps

  1. Meat Sauce: Brown the meat with onion and garlic, then simmer with tomato products and seasonings for about 1.5 hours.
  2. Noodles:  Boil noodles and set aside.
  3. Ricotta Mixture:  Blend ricotta with egg, parsley, and salt.
  4. Layering:  Start with meat sauce, followed by noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and Parmesan. Repeat the layers.
  5. Baking:  Cover with foil, bake at 375Â°F for 25 minutes, uncover and bake for another 25 minutes.
  6. Resting:  Let the Biryani rest for 15 minutes before serving.
